Smoked Pork BBQ

Pulled Smoked Pork Shoulder

Nothing screams summer time like some slow cooked smoked bbq, but this dish is something you can make year round.

This is the perfect dish to make when you are looking to prep food for a large family gathering, neighborhood party, or just looking to cook once and eat for the week.

Ingredients

  • Pork Butt Shoulder
  • Choice of Seasonings
  • Choice of wood flavor for smoking

Steps

  1. Preheat your smoker for 225-250 degrees
  2. Unpack your pork shoulder and season generously (This step can be done the night before to allow the seasoning to set in
  3. Place seasoned shoulder in the smoker and smoke for 45 minutes to an hour per pound
  4. Optionally you can wrap your shoulder once it reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees
  5. Cook shoulder until internal temperature reaches 204 degrees
  6. Pull shoulder off of smoker and wrap (if not done previously) and rest in a cooler for an hour
  7. Use forks or meat claws to pull your pork shoulder
  8. Add additional seasoning to taste and enjoy
