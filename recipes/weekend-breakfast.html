Weekend Breakfast

Sausage, Bacon, Hashbrowns, Eggs and Toast

What kind of breakfast beats the good ole grand slam? While that answer can vary, my go to breakfast on the weekends is nothing short of a full plate.

Golden hashbrowns, juicy sausage, crispy bacon, fresh eggs and some buttered toast starts my weekends off on the right foot and makes sure that my body has the fuel it needs to tackle the weekends work.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Eggs
  • Frozen HashBrowns (You can use whatever type you wish)
  • Vermont Maple Sausage Links or Patties
  • Bacon...I like thick cut, but any bacon will work
  • Sliced bread for toast
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat your griddle
  2. Add a little butter to the griddle
  3. Place your hashbrowns on the buttered area
  4. Add your bacon and sausage to the griddle
  5. Once your bacon, sausage and hasbrowns cooked on one side, flip them and clean an area for your eggs
  6. Add some butter and then crack open your eggs (You can cook them how you like them)
  7. Start plating your hashbrowns, sausage, and bacon
  8. Clean an area of your griddle and place your buttered bread down
  9. Plate your eggs and flip your toast
  10. Plate your toast and turn off your griddle
  11. Enjoy your breakfast with your favorite morning beverage
