Hibachi Recipe

Hibachi Chicken with Rice and Veggies

Hibachi Dinners are traditionally prepped and cooked on a flat griddle surrounded by a bar top table and chairs on three sides.

Customers place their orders and are able to watch the chef cook their meals in front of them.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Chicken, Steak, Shrimp or Combination
  • 1 cup White Rice
  • 1 cup Water
  • Ginger Sauce for color and taste
  • 1 Egg
  • Yum Yum Sauce for color and taste
  • 2 cups Choice of Vegetables (Typically Broccoli, Carrots, Peas, and Zuchinni)

Steps

  1. Prep Your Protiens by cubing your Chicken or Steak and Peeling and Deveining your Shrimp
  2. Cook the White Rice and let cool
  3. Chop up your veggies
  4. Heat up your griddle
  5. Place the cooled rice on the heated griddle
  6. Scramble the egg and mix in with the cooked rice
  7. Add Ginger Sauce and Yum Yum Sauce to rice and egg mix until a light brown color
  8. Add the chopped veggies to the griddle
  9. Start plating your cooked rice
  10. Add your protiens to the griddle
  11. Start plating your veggies
  12. Once the protiens are cooked to the desired doneness, proceed to plating the protiens
  13. Add small portions of Ginger and Yum Yum sauce for dipping to the plates
